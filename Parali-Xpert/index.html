Our Story

___________________

In rural farmlands, farmers often burn leftover crop residue or Parali to clear their fields. While it is quick and easy but this practice harms the environment, causing severe air pollution and smog. At the same time, industries are constantly searching for eco-friendly raw materials. We team IdeaGen saw a simple solution: instead of burning Parali why not sell it to industries through government channels? This way, Parali could be turned into something useful, like biofuel or packaging, benefiting both farmers and industries while protecting the environment. And so,परालीXpert was born—a step towards cleaner air and sustainable solutions......
